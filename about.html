About Us

National Electronics, a subsidiary of Safa Group, is a leading market player in the electronics and cooling systems industry. Based in Mogadishu, Somalia, we are proud to be appointed agents for renowned international brands such as;

  • LG Electronics
  • Super General
  • GROUPSEB (Moulinex Brand)
  • Tefal Brand

    • Our manufacturers offer a wide range of state-of-the-art home appliances and electronics that incorporate the latest efficient technologies and rigorous quality control processes. With a comprehensive inventory of products including air conditioners, washing machines, refrigerators, cookers, lightings, cables, and wires, we aim to be the go-to destination for all electronic devices and home appliances. Additionally, we provide installation, repair, and consultation services for air conditioners and other electronic equipment, and prioritize customer satisfaction through our dedicated after-sales support and guidance.
